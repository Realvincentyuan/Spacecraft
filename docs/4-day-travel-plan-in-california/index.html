4-Day Travel Plan in California

Vincent Yuan · updated on  Aug 21, 2023 · 2 min read
Life

4 days are definitely not enough to experience California in depth, but given limited time, I have decided to make a compact plan to go through the famous attractions in California such as:

  • California State Route 1 and Golden Gate Bridge
  • Universal Studio in Hollywood
  • Silicon Valley

Day 1: Exploring Los Angeles and Surroundings

Morning:

  • Start your day with a visit to the Griffith Observatory for panoramic views of the city and the Hollywood Sign.
  • Griffith Observatory: Official Website

Afternoon:

  • Explore the iconic Santa Monica Pier and its nearby beaches.
  • Santa Monica Pier: Official Website

Evening:

  • Take a leisurely stroll along the Venice Beach Boardwalk to experience the vibrant atmosphere.
  • Venice Beach Boardwalk: Official Website

Day 2: Universal Studios Hollywood

Morning to Evening:

  • Spend a full day at Universal Studios Hollywood, enjoying its attractions, shows, and themed areas.
  • Universal Studios Hollywood: Official Website

Day 3: Travel to San Francisco

Morning:

  • Check out of your Los Angeles accommodation and travel to San Francisco. Consider a flight or scenic drive along the Pacific Coast Highway.

Afternoon:

  • Arrive in San Francisco and check into your hotel.
  • Fisherman's Wharf: Official Website

Day 4: Exploring San Francisco

Morning:

  • Take a ferry to Alcatraz Island and explore the former prison.
  • Alcatraz Island Tours: Official Website

Afternoon:

  • Visit the iconic Golden Gate Bridge and enjoy the views from various vantage points.
  • Golden Gate Bridge: Official Website

Evening:

  • Explore Chinatown and have dinner at a local restaurant.

Remarks

Please note that for some attractions, it's advisable to purchase tickets in advance to secure your spot. Here are the links to buy tickets:

  • Griffith Observatory: Tickets
  • Santa Monica Pier (no specific ticket required, some rides are pay-as-you-go)
  • Universal Studios Hollywood: Tickets
  • Alcatraz Island Tours: Tickets
  • Golden Gate Bridge (no ticket required for bridge visit)

Make sure to check the official websites for the most up-to-date information, ticket prices, and any COVID-19 related guidelines or restrictions that may be in place during your visit. Enjoy your California adventure!
