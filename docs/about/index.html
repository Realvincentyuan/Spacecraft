About this site

Spacecraft is an independent publication launched in July 2023 by Vincent Yuan.

This is a place where we can discuss:

  • Technology and data science knowledge
  • Professional tools and experience sharing
  • Personal stories

If you subscribe today, you'll get full access to the website as well as email newsletters about new content when it's available. Your subscription makes this site possible, and allows Spacecraft to continue to exist. Thank you!

Who am I?

I work as a data scientist in the financial sector, where I specialize in analytics, report generation, and model development for auditing and credit card fraud detection. Some of my passions include:

  • Photography
  • Writing
  • Building projects like this blog
  • Playing tennis

You can frequently find my photos on Unsplash, where they are available for personal use without any copyright concerns.

Why am I doing this?

First and foremost, I love writing because it lets me share my thoughts with the world. It's a way for me to:

  • Learn new things and pass that knowledge on to others in an organized manner.
  • Run and keep up a blog all by myself.
  • Connect with people who share similar interests.

So, in an ideal world, this is beneficial for everyone, and I hope this place thrives for a long time!
