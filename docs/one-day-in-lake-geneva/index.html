On the road

There a few ways to Lake Geneva from Chicago which is where I live. But it turns out that the country road as below is more fun and beautiful compared to the slightly faster I94 route. As throughout the way, you may encounter into nice villages, farms and plains compared to boring concrete on the highway.

Given the time on the road, we decided to start in the morning so that a delicious brunch near Lake Geneva downtown can help kick off the trip, and there are indeed a lot of good restaurants. An American breakfast restaurant Great Eggs was the first stop and they claimed that they make one of the best burritos in those areas, and that was true! You should expect to eat in a small but clean little restaurant and welcome by the friendly people and great food. You are encouraged to just park the car near that place as this is not far away from the lake and it is free.

All about the lake

There are indeed a lot of fun around the lake such as kayak, boat cruise, hiking on the lake shore trails, it was warm when we got there in May 2023, and that was actually the first day that the kayak was re-opened after the long winter. That was a lot of fun, you are encouraged to sail to the remote edge of the lake and go back to the dock. Throughout the sail, the clear water, incredible houses and mansions on the lake shore, and luxurious boats definitely make it worth to spend two hours on paddling, lol.

Remarks
